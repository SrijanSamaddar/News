ISRO's Chandrayaan-4 Mission to Launch in 2026, Says Official

By Srijan Samaddar | July 5, 2025

In an exciting announcement today, the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) confirmed that its next lunar mission, Chandrayaan-4, is scheduled for launch in early 2026.

The announcement has created a wave of excitement among science enthusiasts and citizens alike. With this step, India continues its journey as a space superpower on the global stage.
